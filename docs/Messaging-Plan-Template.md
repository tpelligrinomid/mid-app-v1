> **What goes where — quick reference for filling this in**
>
> - **Differs by client every time:** Market Opportunity, Positioning Claim, all Brand Story fields, Pillars, ICP blocks, Products Matrix, Use/Avoid list.
> - **Often reusable with light edits:** the *structure* of the StoryBrand arc, the explanatory italic prompts, the matrix columns.
> - **Decide per engagement:** whether to include Messaging Pillars + ICP-Specific Messaging (heavier, B2B/advisory-style plans) or lean more on One-Liners + Products Matrix (product/DTC-style plans). Both example plans share the Brand Story spine; they diverge here.
